Barefoot Running as a Neuromuscular Warm-Up

Investigating how a brief barefoot running session recalibrates shod running mechanics and reduces injury risk factors in recreational runners.

Multimodal Python pipeline synchronising high-frequency EMG, 3D motion capture (Vicon), and force plate kinetics, processed through OpenSim musculoskeletal models.

Key results: ×22 increase in gastrocnemius pre-activation, −7% ground contact time, +18% knee flexion at initial contact after barefoot intervention.
